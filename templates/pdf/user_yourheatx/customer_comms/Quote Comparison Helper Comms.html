If you’re currently looking at a few quotes for your new boiler, you’re definitely not alone — most people like to explore a couple of options before making a decision.

If it helps, here are a few things worth checking when comparing quotes:

✔ What’s actually included?
Some quotes may look lower at first, but don’t always include everything needed for a complete installation.

✔ Hot water performance
Not all combi boilers deliver the same level of hot water. It’s worth checking that the system being quoted will give you the performance you’re used to — especially if you have multiple bathrooms or higher demand.

✔ System preparation and setup
Understanding how the system will be prepared — including cleaning and proper setup — can make a big difference to how well everything runs long term.

✔ Warranty length and support
Not all warranties are the same — it’s worth checking what’s covered, how long for, and who you deal with if something goes wrong.

✔ Aftercare and ongoing support
Knowing you’ve got someone to rely on after installation is just as important as the install itself.
